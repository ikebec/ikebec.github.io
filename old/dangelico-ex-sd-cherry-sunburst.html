EX-SD Cherry Sunburst

D'Angelico

Here's a nice guitar for LP enthusiasts looking to try something new. The chambered body gives it a great sense of balance. The hand-friendly neck profile is not too thin yet far from chunky, the frets are low and fast and the neck is straight, making it a very playable instrument.

This particular model has a bookmatched flame maple top and a straight-grain rosewood fingerboard. The inlay pieces chosen along the board are particularly beautiful. High quality hardware keeps this in tune and intonated perfectly.

As for the sound, it bleeds a warm rich tone, great sustain, low action with cut and dressed frets done in-house by myself.

Comes with a hardshell case and a nice leather strap.
