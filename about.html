About Ike's

We are a small family-owned and operated shop with a limited collection of guitars. Everything we carry is something we would be proud to own and play ourselves.

In addition. we also run a full-service repir shop specializing in electronics, fret-work, and custom nut and saddle fabrication.

If you have any questions at all please feel free to call or email. If we don't pick up the phone it's because we are likely doing some repairs or speaking with another customer. Leave a message and your call will be returned promptly.
