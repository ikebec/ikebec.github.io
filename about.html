About the Shop

---

  • > Open shop hours are updated weekly and can be found here: Open Shop Hours. No appointments necessary. Consultations and estimates are free of charge.
  • > Cash and credit cards are accepted. Credit cards are subject to a 3.5% service charge.
  • > Typical turnaround is 2-3 business days except where parts are ordered, in which case shipping times will be estimated and provided.
  • > Rush jobs are available and are subject to a 25% surcharge on labor.
  • > Ordering parts through the shop is highly recommended. All parts provided through the shop are directly from the manufacturer or through authorized distributors and come with a warranty.
  • > When work is completed you will recieve a text message and a link to the upcoming shop hours for you to arrange a convenient time to pick up your instrument.
  • > Ike's is a luthier owned and operated shop. When you visit you will speak to the person who will be working on your instrument. Your playing style, preferences and personal needs are central to determing the scope of work performed.
  • > The shop and all instruments inside are fully insured and all work is warrantied.
