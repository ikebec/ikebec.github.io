About the Shop

---

  • > In order to provide one-on-one service, we operate by appointment only. You can find a time that works for you here: Schedule.
  • > Bench rates start at $100. This is the minimum for all work performed. Estimates are free and non-obligatory.
  • > All parts provided through the shop are directly from the manufacturer or through authorized distributors.
  • > Typical turnaround is 1-2 weeks.
  • > When work is completed you will recieve an email with a link to the schedule for you to arrange a convenient time to pick up your instrument.
  • > Cash, Zelle and Venmo are accepted.
  • > Ike's is a luthier owned and operated shop. When you visit you will speak to the person who will be working on your instrument. Your playing style, preferences and personal needs are central to determing the scope of work performed.
  • > The shop and all instruments inside are fully insured and all work is warrantied.
