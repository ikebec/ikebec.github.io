Repair Services

 
  • Estimates are provided in person and free of charge.
  • We supply our shop with high quality parts from reputable manufacturers and distributors. While you are free to bring your own parts, we can only warranty parts that we supply and recommend you get them through us. We carry D'Addario, Ernie Ball and Elixer strings.
  • Luthier owned and operated. The shop and all instruments inside are fully insured and all work is warrantied.
  • Re-strings - Clean board and re-string guitar with your choice of strings.
    •  
  • Set-ups - Adjust the relief in the neck, cut the nut slots to the correct width, depth and angle, make sure the saddle is fitted flush to the bottom of the bridge saddle slot for acoustic instruments, adjust action and intonation at the saddle, polish the frets and dress the ends as necessary, wax or oil the board, clean all electronics and tighten hardware.
    •  
  • Refrets - Replace all or some of the frets where there is limited fret height from previous leveling or from heavy play wear. In instances where all frets are removed, the board will be leveled and radiused as necessary prior to new frets being installed. We offer traditional nickel silver, EVO gold and stainless steel wire.
    •  
  • Fret Dress - In leiu of a refret, level and crown frets where there is ample fret remaining resulting in clean notes across the board.
    •  
  • Pickup Installation - We are authorized dealers for Seymour Duncan, Dimarzio, LR Baggs, and K&K.
    •  
  • Nut and Saddle Fabrication - We use bone (bleached or unbleached) and micarta when period correct and requested.
    •  
  • Binding repair and replacement - Reglue loose binding that has separated from the body. When binding has broken off or separated too dramatically for reattachment, we will remove and replace with the preferred material.
    •  
  • Electronics Repair and Modifications - Our preferences are for vintage-style electronics. We use high quality pots, wiring and capacitors in our work. We do not work on circuit boards or create custom wiring diagrams.
    •  
  • Structural and Crack Repair - Cracks are aligned, glued and cleated for lasting structural integrity.
    •  
  • Bridge Repair and Fabrication - In situations where the bridge has separated from the top, as happens from top warpage or string tension over time, the bridge is removed, refitted and reglued. If necessary a new bridge can be fabicated from rosewood or ebony to match the correct style and size.
    •  
  • Bridge Plate Repair - Over time, bridge plates can wear. When possible we will plug the holes in the plate with the correct maple or rosewood depending on the original material and redrill for a clean and lasting anchor for the ball ends of the strings.
    •  
  • Neck Resets - Offered on Martin acoustic guitars built with a dovetail joint. When there is less than ideal saddle height and the action on the instrument is too high, a neck reset may be the recommended course of action. This involves removing the neck, resetting the angle in the dovetail joint, and reassembling. In general we use traditional hide glues.
