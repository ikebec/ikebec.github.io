We are a family-owned guitar supply shop specializing in high-quality parts from our favorite manufacturers. Located in the Highland Park neighborhood of Los Angeles, our shop aims to be a place for musicians to find quality parts and pedals with thoughtful customer service.

 

Currently we carry Leo Quan, Tremol-No, Danelectro Pedals, JLD Research Bridge Systems, MXR, Schaller and Gotoh parts.

Proud members of The Guild of American Luthiers, The Association of Stringed Instrument Artisans (ASIA) and NAMM.
